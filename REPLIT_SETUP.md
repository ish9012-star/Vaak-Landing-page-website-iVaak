# Vaak Landing Page - Replit Black Friday Setup Guide 🎉

## Quick 10-Minute Setup with Replit's Black Friday Offer

This guide will help you deploy your Vaak landing page and claim your free .com domain using Replit's Black Friday promotion.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Replit Account (2 minutes)

1. Go to [Replit.com](https://replit.com)
2. Sign up with Google, GitHub, or email
3. Verify your email address

---

## Step 2: Claim Black Friday Offer (2 minutes)

1. Click on the Black Friday banner in Replit
2. Subscribe to **Replit Core** 
3. Benefit: Get a **free .com domain** (worth $12-15/year)
4. Complete payment setup

> **Note**: If you miss the Black Friday offer, Replit still provides free hosting at `your-site.your-username.repl.co` which works great for beta testing!

---

## Step 3: Create New Repl (1 minute)

1. Click **"+ Create Repl"** in your dashboard
2. Select **"HTML, CSS, JS"** template
3. Name it: `vaak-landing` or `vaak`
4. Click **"Create Repl"**

---

## Step 4: Upload Your Files (2 minutes)

### Option A: Upload via Replit Interface

1. In the file explorer (left sidebar), delete these default files:
   - `index.html`
   - `style.css`
   - `script.js`

2. Click the **three dots** (⋮) at top of file explorer
3. Select **"Upload file"**
4. Upload these files one by one:
   - `index.html`
   - `styles.css`
   - `script.js`

### Option B: Copy-Paste Content

1. Create new file: Click **"+ Add file"**
2. Name it exactly: `index.html`
3. Copy entire content from your `index.html` and paste
4. Repeat for `styles.css` and `script.js`

---

## Step 5: Configure Email for Beta Signups (2 minutes)

1. Open `script.js` in Replit editor
2. Press `Ctrl+F` (Windows) or `Cmd+F` (Mac)
3. Search for: `YOUR_EMAIL@gmail.com`
4. Replace with your actual email
5. Press `Ctrl+S` (Windows) or `Cmd+S` (Mac) to save

**Example:**
```javascript
// Before:
await fetch('https://formsubmit.co/ajax/YOUR_EMAIL@gmail.com', {

// After:
await fetch('https://formsubmit.co/ajax/ishleen@vaak.app', {
```

> **Important**: Use an email you check regularly. Beta signups will be sent here!

---

## Step 6: Test Your Site (1 minute)

1. Click the green **"Run"** button at the top
2. Your site will open in the preview pane
3. Scroll through all sections to check everything looks good
4. Click **"Open in new tab"** icon to view full screen

### Quick Test Checklist:
- [ ] All sections visible and styled correctly
- [ ] Navigation links work (click "Features", "How It Works", etc.)
- [ ] Phone mockup displays properly
- [ ] Beta signup form is visible

## Step 7: Test Beta Signup Form (2 minutes)

1. Scroll to "Be Among the First 100" section
2. Fill out the form with TEST data:
   - Name: Test User
   - Email: your-email@gmail.com (use your real email)
   - Platform: iOS, Android, or Both
3. Click **"Join Beta Waitlist"**
4. You should see success message: "Welcome to the Vaak Family! 🙏"
5. Check your email (including spam folder) for the signup notification

> **Note**: The first FormSubmit.co email may take 5-10 minutes and might go to spam. After first submission, mark as "Not Spam" for future signups.

---

## Step 8: Claim Your Free .com Domain (3 minutes)

If you subscribed to Replit Core:

1. Click on your Repl settings (gear icon) in top right
2. Navigate to **"Domains"** section
3. Click **"Add custom domain"**
4. Search for your desired domain:
   - `vaak.com` (if available)
   - `getvak.com`
   - `vaakapp.com`
   - `dailyvaak.com`
5. Click **"Register"** on available domain (FREE with Replit Core)
6. Fill in domain registration details
7. Click **"Complete registration"**

### Domain will be live in 5-15 minutes!

**SSL Certificate**: Replit automatically provides free HTTPS for your domain ✅

---

## Step 9: Make Your Site Public (1 minute)

1. Click **"Share"** button (top right)
2. Ensure **"Public"** is selected
3. Copy your site link (either custom domain or repl.co URL)
4. Your site is now live! 🎉

**Share your link:**
- `https://vaak.com` (if you got custom domain)
- OR `https://vaak-landing.your-username.repl.co`

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue: Form submissions not arriving

**Solution:**
1. Check spam folder
2. Verify email in `script.js` is correct (no typos!)
3. Wait 5-10 minutes for first submission
4. Check browser console (F12) for error messages

### Issue: Styles look broken

**Solution:**
1. Ensure `styles.css` filename is exact (lowercase 's')
2. Check all three files are in root directory
3. Click "Run" button again
4. Clear browser cache and refresh

### Issue: Domain not working

**Solution:**
1. Wait 15 minutes (DNS propagation)
2. Try accessing with `https://` prefix
3. Check domain settings in Replit
4. Contact Replit support if issue persists

### Issue: "Run" button shows errors

**Solution:**
1. Check file names are exact:
   - `index.html` (not Index.html)
   - `styles.css` (not style.css or Styles.css)
   - `script.js` (not Script.js)
2. Ensure all files have content
3. Look at console output for specific error
